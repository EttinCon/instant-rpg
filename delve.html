Delve the Depths

Delve Move

When you explore a perilous site in search of an objective, select the theme and domain which best represent its nature (Ask the Oracle if unsure), and set its rank.
  • Troublesome site: 3 progress per area.
  • Dangerous site: 2 progress per area.
  • Formidable site: 1 progress per area.
  • Extreme site: 2 ticks per area.
  • Epic site: 1 tick per area.

If you are returning to a previously explored site, roll your challenge dice, take the lowest value, and clear that number of progress boxes.

Then, for each area you traverse, envision your surroundings (Ask the Oracle if unsure). If you navigate this area…

  • With haste, roll +edge
  • With stealth or trickery, roll +shadow
  • With observation, intuition, or expertise, roll +wits

On a strong hit, you delve deeper. Mark progress and Discover an Opportunity.

On a weak hit, roll on the following according to your stat.

Edge Shadow Wits Result
1-34 1-13 1-23 Mark progress, but also Reveal a Danger.
35-65 14-64 24-53 Mark progress.
66-75 65-85 54-77 Choose one: mark progress or Discover an Opportunity.
76-80 86-95 78-95 Take both: mark progress and Discover an Opportunity.
81-00 96-00 96-00 Mark +1 progress.
On a miss, Reveal a Danger.

Discover an Opportunity

Delve Move

When you encounter a helpful situation within a site, roll on the following. If you are making this move as a result of a strong hit on Delve the Depths, you may choose or envision an opportunity instead of rolling.

If you make a move which directly leverages this advantage, you may (one time only) add +1 and take +1 momentum on a hit.

Roll Result
1-2You cross paths with a person, creature or faction who is inclined to support you.
3-5You encounter a person, creature or faction in need of help.
6-8You encounter an area or object of mystic or religious significance.
9-18You locate a secure area.
19-28This area provides an opportunity to scavenge, forage, or hunt.
29-38A clue offers insight into your quest.
39-48You get the drop on a potential foe.
49-69You encounter something which offers insight into the history or nature of this place.
70-100The terrain favors you, or you find a hidden path.

Locate Your Objective

Delve (Progress) Move

When your exploration of a site comes to an end, roll the challenge dice and compare to your progress. Momentum is ignored on this roll.

On a strong hit, you locate your objective and the situation favors you. Choose one.

  • Make another move now (not a progress move), and add +1.
  • Take +1 momentum.

On a weak hit, you locate your objective but face an unforeseen hazard or complication. Envision what you find (Ask the Oracle if unsure).

On a miss, your objective falls out of reach, you have been misled about the nature of your objective, or you discover that this site holds unexpected depths. If you continue your exploration, clear all but one filled progress and raise the site’s rank by one (if not already epic). Otherwise, Escape the Depths and do what you must to find a path forward.

Escape the Depths

Delve Move

When you flee or withdraw from a site, roll +wits.

On a strong hit, you make your way safely out of this place. Take +1 momentum.

On a weak hit, you find your way out, but this place exacts its price. You and your allies must each choose one.

  • You are worn and weary: Endure Harm (2 harm)
  • The experience leaves you shaken: Endure Stress (2 stress)
  • You are delayed: Suffer -2 momentum.

On a miss, this place hinders your departure. You must Reveal a Danger. If you survive, you may attempt to Escape the Depths again.

Check your Gear

Delve Move

When you check to see if you have a specific helpful item, roll +supply.

On a strong hit, you have it. Take +2 momentum.

On a weak hit, you have it, but your resources are diminished. Take +1 momentum and suffer -1 supply.

On a miss, you don’t have it and the situation grows more perilous. Pay the Price.
