INSTANT RPG - by @WanderingGM -

Just Add Players!

This is a role-playing game for 2 or more people, requiring no dice or paper, using Rock/Paper/Scissors to resolve uncertain outcomes.

One player runs the game (swipe for more), the others play characters in a story which you tell together.

When the player running the game challenges you with Rock/Paper/Scissors, there are 3 possible outcomes:

If you win, you get what you want. Something good happens for you and your friends.

If you tie, you get what you want, but something bad happens to you, your fellow players, or just in general.

If you lose, you don’t get what you want, and something really bad happens.

To start the game, players answer the following questions, alongside any clarifying questions which the answers may inspire:

  • Where do you imagine we are?
  • Why do you imagine we are here?
  • Who do you imagine trying to stop us?

Rock the Boat. When a player tries something risky or introduces a story element, challenge them with Rock/Paper/Scissors, describing the results.

Share the Spotlight. Give everyone equal opportunity to contribute to the story. Choose a new player, then ask them:

“What do you do?” The most important question you can ask. When you finish describing things, always ask someone else “What do you do?”

Never Say No. If you think it doesn’t fit in the story, challenge them. Stop the game if needed. It’s much more important to keep friendships than to play a silly game.

Go Wild. The stories one can tell with *INSTANT RPG* are very short (an hour at most), so the story will (and should) go off the rails.

Champion the Players. They lead the story. Celebrate their victories & lament their failures, you are gathered here to watch a story unfold before your very eyes.

Bowing Out. When you decide to stop playing (or must), describe a satisfactory ending or a tantalising cliffhanger!
